BpA Architecture specializing in custom home design and various residential projects and commercial
projects has been practicing since 2004.

Bruce Anchell, Principal of the company, is a registered Architect in the state of Florida and has over
30 years of professional experience in the field of Architecture. BpA Architecture prides itself on
transforming a client’s vision, ideas and goals into reality. BpA Architecture’s exceptional
professional design services and attention to detail assures their projects are on time and
constructible.

Bruce has earned degrees from the University of Florida (Bachelor of Design) and University of Miami
(Bachelor of Architecture) and is a certified LEED AP (Sustainable Design)

Bruce enjoys spending time with his wonderful family: Wife Ilene and daughters Andi and Beth.
